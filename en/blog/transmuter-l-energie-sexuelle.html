Maintain and cultivate positive thinking

To manifest your desires, first keep a high level of daily joy and enthusiasm.

The most effective mental stimulants to vibrate, create, and expand imagination, in order, are:

  1. Sexual desire
  2. Love
  3. A strong desire for recognition, power, or gain
  4. Music
  5. Friendship (same or different sex)
  6. A mastermind group
  7. Shared suffering (e.g., a persecuted people)
  8. Autosuggestion
  9. Fear
  10. Narcotics and alcohol

Genius, sixth sense, and creative imagination

A genius learns to increase the intensity of thought until it freely connects to sources beyond ordinary thinking.

He or she knows there is a sixth sense—the creative sexual energy. This sixth sense is what distinguishes a genius from the average person.

Ideas come from four sources:

  1. Infinite Intelligence
  2. The subconscious, where impulses that bypass the five senses are stored
  3. Another person’s conscious mind releasing an image or idea
  4. The subconscious of others

Trust your creative energy. Express the ideas whispered by your inner voice. Close your eyes and let inspiration move through you.

Work with imagination—both synthetic and creative. Known ideas + insights from experience.

The genius approach

  1. Using a chosen stimulant, the inventor elevates the mind to a higher plane.
  2. He focuses on known elements and forms a clear image of the unknown parts.
  3. He holds the image until the subconscious takes over, then relaxes and awaits the response.
  4. Results may be precise, immediate, or absent—depending on creative energy development.

Personal magnetism

“Sex desire is the most powerful of human desires. When harnessed and redirected, it fuels imagination, courage, will, perseverance and great creative capacity.” — Napoleon Hill

The urge for physical contact can be transmuted into another form of desire or action. Don’t let sexual energy spill unconsciously.

You sense a person’s magnetism in:

  1. Handshake
  2. Tone of voice (warm, musical)
  3. Gestures and gait (vitality, grace, ease)
  4. Style of dress
  5. Thought waves (direct this energy to influence your thinking)

To cultivate genius: learn to develop, channel, and use sexual desire and Love.

The process:

  1. In your mind, give precedence to constructive emotions; discourage destructive ones.
  2. Understand transmutation: a negative emotion can be transformed into a positive one.
  3. Immerse yourself in the emotion of Love by recalling a vivid memory of it.

Use the strategic calendar

Any time you practice sexual manifestation, you may move something forward. Still, certain moments are strategic. New moon favors intention and abundance; full moon supports release. Use both.

Full moon: release what holds you back. Let sexual energy cleanse. Water (a shower) or rain helps symbolically wash away residue.

Waning phases: reflect on what you let go. Keep making space for the new. During sex, let each orgasm release what no longer serves you.

New moon: best time to set intention and build. Create a meaningful space, partner if you wish, and choose a clear intention. Prefer slow, stable, long‑term experiences.

Waxing phases: keep creating and acting consistently. Commitment is key.
